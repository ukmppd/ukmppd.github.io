    Hemoroid

    Definisi

    Hemoroid adalah bantalan vaskular normal yang digantung dalam lapisan submukosa saluran anal oleh jaringan konektif longitudinal dan serat otot. Hemoroid menjadi patologis ketika membengkak atau meradang. Hemoroid internal berasal dari atas garis dentate, sementara hemoroid eksternal terjadi di bawah level ini. Hemoroid komponen campuran dapat terjadi di atas dan di bawah garis dentate.1,2,3 Hemorrhoids are cushions of highly vascular structures found within the submucosa of the anal canal. They become pathologic when swollen or inflamed. Hemorrhoids are enlarged veins in the anus or lower rectum. They often go unnoticed and usually clear up after a few days, but can cause long-lasting discomfort, bleeding and excruciatingly pain. Effective medical treatments are available.2 Hemorrhoids are normal vascular cushions suspended in the submucosal layer of the anal canal by longitudinal connective tissue and muscle fibers. Mixed component hemorrhoids can occur above and below the dentate line.
